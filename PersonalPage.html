About Me

My name is Winston Hibbert. I have lived in Kingston, Jamaica all my life. Some of my favorite things to do are playing games, listening to music, going on adventurous trips and taking photographs. My career aspirations are to become either a Web Designer or to become a Professional Photographer which I have already started venturing into. I have been told that I am a charming, chill an extremely quiet person who is very observing. As per the emotional side of things about me, I tend to withhold my emotions and not publicly show much as I deal with situations much better alone. I like football, nature, seafood (favorite dish is Lobster), while my dislikes are people taking advantage of me, traffic, and negative people.

I look up mostly to Denzel Washington as a guiing figure in life. Favorite shows of mine include:

  • The Equalizer 1 & 2
  • Man on Fire
  • Inside Man
  • Deja Vu

These are just a few things about yours truly.
